About Me

I started teaching as a 5th grade teacher 20 years ago, but then I moved to teach high school. I wanted a little bit of a challenge so I chose to work at Heated High. The students here are not what I am use to, but I have adapted to them and I have seen them improve as students and people. This is my 6th year at Heated High and I can truthfully say, they have been very interesting, but I would not trade them for anything.
